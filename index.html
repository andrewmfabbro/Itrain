Who We Are

We are creating SMART interval training

iTrain is a individualized athletic development and personal training tool designed for use by both athletes and trainers. The iTrain system is made up of one or more portable buttons that are directly connect to our web application. The button(s) can be adhered to a variety of surfaces and/or can be carried by users.

Optimal athletic development begins by pushing the first button to record the start time and the second button to record the stop time. This gives athletes the ability to record their own interval times with percise accuracy. After a workout, look on our web application to review the amount and duration of intervals.
